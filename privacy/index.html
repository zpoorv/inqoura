Inqoura

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Inqoura collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Inqoura mobile application and related services.

Inqoura is designed to help people review packaged food products by scanning barcodes and ingredient labels, viewing product insights, and tracking shopping patterns over time.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Inqoura, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information, such as your email address, display name, unique user ID, and sign-in provider details when you create an account or sign in with email or Google.
  • Profile and preference data, such as your name, diet profile, appearance mode, app look, share card style, country code, household shopping profiles, favorites, comparison selections, and history notification settings.
  • Scan and history data, such as scanned barcodes, product results, score details, product comparisons, favorites, and timestamps for items you scan or save.
  • Ingredient label OCR text and related parsing signals generated from photos you choose to take or import for ingredient scanning.
  • Subscription and entitlement data, such as your premium status, active product identifier, and subscription management metadata returned by our billing tools.
  • Product issue reports you submit, including report reason, product information, score confidence, and if you are signed in, your email address, display name, and user ID.
  • Device-local notification scheduling state used to avoid duplicate history nudges on the same device.

Information We Do Not Intentionally Upload During Normal Scanning

During normal use, ingredient label photos are processed on-device for OCR. Inqoura does not intentionally upload the original ingredient photo to our database as part of normal scanning. Product images shown in the app may come from third-party product catalogs or Inqoura admin overrides.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To authenticate users and support email verification, password reset, and Google sign-in.
  • To look up products, score them, explain results, and personalize product analysis.
  • To save your scan history, favorites, comparisons, and app preferences across devices.
  • To manage subscriptions, entitlements, restores, and customer billing support.
  • To show rewarded ads when applicable and to measure subscription or ad access state.
  • To schedule local shopping recap notifications when you enable them.
  • To review and improve product data through issue reports and admin corrections.
  • To support, secure, maintain, and improve the app.

Third-Party Services We Use

Inqoura uses third-party services to operate key parts of the app:

  • Firebase, for authentication and cloud data such as user profiles, scan history, admin configuration, and issue reports.
  • Google Sign-In, when you choose to sign in with your Google account.
  • RevenueCat, to manage subscription offerings, purchases, entitlement status, restores, paywalls, and customer billing flows.
  • Google Play Billing or app store billing systems, which process purchases and payment transactions. We do not receive your full payment card details.
  • Google AdMob, when rewarded ads are enabled, to serve ads and related advertising requests.
  • Open Food Facts, for barcode-based product information such as product names, ingredients, nutrition fields, and product images.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information in these limited ways:

  • With the service providers listed above, to run core app functions.
  • With app store billing providers to support subscriptions and restores.
  • With our admin review tools when you submit a product issue report.
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights and safety.
  • As part of a merger, acquisition, or transfer of the service, if that occurs.

Notifications

History recap and shopping nudge notifications are scheduled locally on your device. At this time, Inqoura does not rely on remote push tokens for these notifications. You can turn notifications off at any time in the app or in your device settings.

Data Retention

We keep account, profile, scan history, favorites, comparison selections, and issue reports for as long as needed to provide the service or until you delete your account, subject to legal, security, and fraud-prevention needs.

Some app settings and history-related state may also be stored locally on your device.

Account Deletion

If you use an account, you can request deletion from within the app. Account deletion is intended to remove your local app data and your associated cloud profile and scan history from our Firebase project, although some billing, legal, security, or backup records may be retained where necessary.

You can also request account deletion outside the app at inqoura.app/delete-account.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You can update your profile and preferences inside the app.
  • You can sign out or delete your account from the settings area.
  • You can disable local notifications from app settings or device settings.
  • You can manage subscriptions through the applicable app store or Customer Center.
  • Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain personal data. Contact us to make a request.

Children’s Privacy

Inqoura is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will review the request.

Health and Shopping Guidance

Inqoura provides general shopping and nutrition-related guidance only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional healthcare advice. Always review product packaging directly for the latest ingredient, allergen, and nutrition details.

International Use

Inqoura may be used globally, and data may be processed in countries other than your own depending on the infrastructure used by our service providers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Material changes may also be communicated through the app or other appropriate channels.
