Delete Your Account

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This page explains how Inqoura users can request deletion of their account and associated data.

Delete Inside the App

  1. Open Inqoura and sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request in the app.

Delete Outside the App

If you cannot access the app, email your deletion request to support@inqoura.app from the email address linked to your Inqoura account.

Include the subject line Inqoura Account Deletion Request and mention the email address associated with your account.

What We Delete

  • Your Inqoura account profile.
  • Your synced scan history.
  • Your saved preferences, favorites, and comparison selections associated with that account.
  • Your local app data on the device when deletion is initiated from within the app.

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited records where needed for billing support, fraud prevention, security, legal compliance, or backup recovery. If data must be retained for these reasons, it will be retained only as long as necessary.

Subscriptions

Deleting your Inqoura account does not automatically cancel an active subscription. Manage or cancel subscriptions through Google Play or the billing management tools available to you before or after requesting account deletion.

Questions

If you need help, contact support@inqoura.app.

You can also review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
